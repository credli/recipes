Ingredients:
 - 4 cups boiled chickpeas, drained and mashed
 - 1/2 cup Tahini paste
 - 1 whole lemon juice
 - 1 tablespoon salt
 - 1/4 cup water
 - 2 garlic cloves, smashed
 - Olive oil, virgin cold pressed

Directions:
 1. In a large bowl mix the garlic with salt, lemon, water and tahini to form a paste.

 2. Add the chickpeas paste into the bowl and mix well with tahini paste.

 3. Serve in a deep dish and drizzle with oil.
